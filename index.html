Ornstein
The Dragon Slayer

In Game

Dragon Slayer Ornstein is the main boss from anor londo. Ornstein is found guarding the Lordvessal
with Executioner Smough. Ornsteins moves are quick with only his thrust attacks
having somewhat of a long charge time. Ornstein can shoot bolts of lightning from his Dragon Slayer Spear.
If the Chosen Undead chooses to beat Exectutioner Smough 1st, then Ornstein will absorb his power, which makes him bigger
, stronger, and adds more attacks to his already deadly array of moves.

Lore

Ornstein is believed to be the captain of the 4 knights of gwyn. His Dragon Slayer Spear is blessed with the power of lightning.
It is said that Ornstein left the anor londo in search of the Nameless King(lord gywns 1st born). It is unknown whether Ornstein wanted to
to fight, or serve him.
